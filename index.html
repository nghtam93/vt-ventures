About Us

VT Venturea was established in 2021 by Mr. Nguyen Vu and Mr. Robert Tran and associates. An open investment fund model in the field of Blockchain technology and digital currency finance with the mission of supporting and providing investment information to the community.
“VT Ventures is confident in all investment and development plans for digital innovation 4.0.
Our operations team are people with many years of experience in the field of traditional financial investment and
cryptocurrencies.”

Vision

VT Ventures is oriented to develop into a leading investment fund in the region, constantly innovating and creating to create a sustainable and long-term investment community, minimizing risks in the investment process.

Mission

VT Ventures brings the most prestigious safe investment solutions and opportunities, together building a rich community in the Crypto market.
